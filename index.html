About Me

I am an undegraduate student at Universidad de Buenos Aires.

My licenciate thesis, currently in progress, is concerned with equivariant and graded versions of algebraic bivariant hermitian $K$-theory and their relation with the classification problem for Leavitt path algebras. My advisor is Guillermo Cortiñas.
